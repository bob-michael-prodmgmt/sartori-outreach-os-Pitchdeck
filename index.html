Customer Insight

What users love. What we've learned.

Favorite feature: Cover Letter Engine

Users consistently say the AI cover letter is the feature that "feels like magic." It doesn't just swap company names — it synthesizes their actual experience into a narrative that addresses the specific role. One click, one PDF, genuinely personalized.

"I was sending the same cover letter everywhere with the company name swapped. Now each one actually references my relevant projects. I got three callbacks in my first week."

What we dislike about our own product

The form-filling agent doesn't yet handle every ATS portal perfectly. Workday and Greenhouse are solid; some custom portals need fallback to assisted mode. We ship improvements weekly.

Biggest learnings from users

Strategy > Volume

Users who apply to fewer, higher-fit roles with personalized materials outperform spray-and-pray by 5x on callback rate. Our fit scoring reinforces this.

Senior Professionals Are Underserved

Directors, VPs, and C-suite candidates hate applying through portals. They're our most engaged users — the time savings per application is highest for complex profiles.

The Pipeline Is the Product

Users who track applications and review analytics are 3x more likely to land interviews. The CRM view transformed job searching from chaos into a manageable process.

Technical Judgment

Engineering principles — not a recipe

AI-Native Architecture

Every layer is designed for LLM integration from day one — not bolted on. The AI doesn't sit on top of the product; it is the product. Multi-model strategy optimizes for cost, speed, and quality at each stage.

Intelligent Form Agent

Our proprietary agent doesn't pattern-match or use brittle selectors. It reasons about what each field is asking and maps the right answer from the user's profile. This is the core IP — and it gets smarter with every application.

Parallelized Intelligence Pipeline

Multiple AI stages run concurrently, not sequentially. What others do in 30 seconds, we do in under 10. Speed is a feature when you're filling out your 50th application.

Single-Tenant Data, Multi-Model AI

User data never leaves their account boundary. AI models are called with ephemeral context — no fine-tuning on user data, no cross-contamination. Privacy by architecture.

Built for Scale from Day One

Async-first backend, connection pooling, containerized deployment, and infrastructure-as-code. The architecture handles 10 users or 100,000 without a rewrite.

Proprietary Scoring Engine

Evidence-based fit scoring that goes beyond keyword matching. Weighted multi-dimensional analysis across skills, experience, seniority, location, and compensation. Learns from outcomes.

Founder's technical depth

I code every day. 8,500+ lines of production code shipped in the last sprint alone. 120+ API endpoints, 50+ frontend pages — built by one person in 12 weeks using AI-augmented development.

Prior: 7 years shipping enterprise AI products at IBM Watson. M.S. from Carnegie Mellon in AI/Data Science. 2 US patents. This isn't my first AI system — it's my best one.
